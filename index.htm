Kevin A. Lai

I'm a fifth year PhD student in the CS/ACO program at Georgia Tech, advised by Jake Abernethy. I received my B.S. at Yale and my M.S.E. at Princeton.

My work is in theoretical machine learning, with a focus on optimization and online learning. I interned at Google Research in NYC in the summer of 2018.
